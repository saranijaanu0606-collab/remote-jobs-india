About Us

Connecting India's top talent with remote-first startups

Our Mission

Remote Startup Jobs India exists to make remote work accessible to every talented professional across India — not just those in Bangalore or Mumbai.

We curate verified remote roles from India's fastest-growing startups so you spend less time searching and more time building your career.

Why We Built This

Finding genuine remote jobs at Indian startups was frustratingly hard. Most job boards mix in hybrid roles or international listings that don't fit India's context.

We built a focused, no-noise board specifically for remote roles at India-based startups — with real salary ranges in LPA, not USD.
